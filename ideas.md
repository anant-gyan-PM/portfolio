**Design Movement**: Swiss Rationalism meets Digital Brutalism

**Core Principles**:
- Radical clarity through extreme typographic hierarchy
- Asymmetric grid systems that challenge conventional balance
- Functional minimalism with unexpected bold accents
- Data-first presentation with metrics as visual anchors

**Color Philosophy**: Monochromatic foundation (charcoal, slate, off-white) punctuated by a single electric accent (neon cyan or safety orange). The restraint amplifies authority while the accent signals innovation and disruption—perfect for a PM who drives measurable impact.

**Layout Paradigm**: Broken grid with intentional misalignment. Large typographic blocks anchor sections, metrics float in unexpected positions, and whitespace creates dramatic pauses. Content flows in columns of varying widths, creating visual tension that demands attention.

**Signature Elements**:
- Oversized metric displays (e.g., "$3M" in 120px font)
- Diagonal section dividers cutting across the viewport
- Monospace font for all numerical data and metrics

**Interaction Philosophy**: Minimal but purposeful. Hover states reveal additional context through subtle overlays. Scroll-triggered animations bring metrics into view with precision timing. No gratuitous motion—every animation serves clarity.

**Animation**: Staggered fade-ins for metric cards (100ms delay between each), diagonal wipes for section transitions, and subtle parallax on hero elements. All animations use cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1) for professional easing.

**Typography System**: 
- Display: Space Grotesk (700) for headlines and section titles
- Body: IBM Plex Sans (400, 500) for readable content
- Data: JetBrains Mono (500) for all metrics and numbers
- Scale: 14px base, 18px body, 32px subheadings, 56px+ hero

**Design Movement**: Neo-Modernist Editorial Design

**Core Principles**:
- Magazine-quality layouts with editorial sophistication
- Generous whitespace as a luxury signal
- Serif-sans contrast for intellectual authority
- Subtle motion that feels organic, not mechanical

**Color Philosophy**: Warm neutrals (cream, taupe, soft charcoal) with deep forest green as the primary accent and burnt sienna for emphasis. This palette conveys maturity, trustworthiness, and strategic thinking—moving away from tech's typical blues while maintaining professionalism.

**Layout Paradigm**: Multi-column editorial grid inspired by high-end publications. Content flows in 2-3 column layouts with pull quotes, sidebar metrics, and full-bleed imagery. Sections have distinct vertical rhythm with generous padding between content blocks.

**Signature Elements**:
- Serif pull quotes highlighting key achievements
- Floating metric badges with soft shadows
- Full-width image treatments with text overlays using blend modes

**Interaction Philosophy**: Smooth and refined. Elements respond to interaction with gentle scale transforms and opacity shifts. Scroll reveals content with elegant fade-up animations. Cursor interactions feel tactile and responsive without being flashy.

**Animation**: Smooth scroll-linked parallax on background elements, fade-up with slight vertical translation (20px) on content reveal, and gentle hover lifts (4px) with shadow expansion on cards. All timing at 600-800ms for luxurious feel.

- Display: Playfair Display (700) for headlines and hero
- Subheadings: Lora (600) for section titles
- Body: Inter (400, 500) for clean readability
- Accents: Lora (italic) for pull quotes and emphasis

**Core Principles**:
- Dynamic energy through strategic use of gradients and glow effects
- Layered depth with frosted glass morphism
- Forward-looking aesthetics that signal innovation
- Confidence through bold scale and dramatic contrast

**Color Philosophy**: Deep midnight blue foundation (oklch(0.15 0.04 260)) with electric violet (oklch(0.65 0.25 290)) and cyan (oklch(0.70 0.15 210)) gradients. Accents of bright lime (oklch(0.85 0.20 130)) for CTAs. This palette projects technical sophistication, strategic vision, and cutting-edge thinking.

**Layout Paradigm**: Floating card-based architecture with overlapping layers. Hero section features a large angular card that breaks the grid. Subsequent sections use offset cards with backdrop blur, creating depth through z-axis stacking. Metrics appear in glowing accent boxes.

**Signature Elements**:
- Gradient mesh backgrounds with subtle animation
- Frosted glass cards with backdrop-blur and border glow
- Animated gradient text for key metrics and headlines

**Interaction Philosophy**: Responsive and alive. Cards tilt subtly on hover (3D transform), buttons pulse with gradient shifts, and scroll triggers reveal elements with spring-based animations. The interface feels like it's responding to user presence.

**Animation**: Spring physics for all interactions (react-spring style), gradient position shifts on hover, staggered card reveals with 3D rotation, and ambient gradient animation in backgrounds (20s loop). Micro-interactions use 300-400ms timing with ease-out-back.

**Typography System**:
- Display: Outfit (700, 800) for bold, geometric headlines
- Body: DM Sans (400, 500) for modern readability
- Accents: Outfit (600) for subheadings and labels
- Monospace: Fira Code (500) for technical details and metrics
- Scale: 15px base, 17px body, 28px subheadings, 72px hero with tight leading
